## Fluency means spelling without guessing

A scale is easier to remember when you treat it as a pattern of letters and steps, not as a random list of keys. Keep one version of each letter name in order, then place accidentals where the scale type requires them.

<NotationFigure title="G major scale" notation="G4/8, A4, B4, C5, D5, E5, F#5, G5" />

<Callout title="Two checks at once" tone="melody">
  First check the letter path. Then check the half-step locations. That prevents spellings like G A B C D E Gb G in a major scale.
</Callout>

## Minor has more than one classroom form

Natural minor is the plain minor collection. Harmonic minor raises scale degree 7 for stronger pull back to tonic. Melodic minor often raises 6 and 7 while ascending in common-practice descriptions.
