## Diatonic means inside the key

In C major, a diatonic triad uses notes from C D E F G A B. Build a triad on each scale degree by skipping through that same collection.

<Callout title="Roman numerals show place and quality" tone="harmony">
  Uppercase numerals often mark major chords. Lowercase numerals often mark minor chords. In C major, I is C major and ii is D minor.
</Callout>

## Three beginner functions

Tonic chords feel like home. Dominant chords point back toward home. Predominant chords often prepare the dominant. These are listening labels, not rules that every song must obey.
