## Analysis is organized noticing

Start with visible facts: key, meter, repeated sections, chord roots, and cadences. Then connect them into a short explanation of how the music moves.

<Callout title="Keep analysis small" tone="harmony">
  One good sentence about a four-bar idea is better than a long label list that does not explain anything.
</Callout>
