## Voices are lines inside chords

Voice-leading asks how each chord tone moves to the next chord. A smooth path often keeps shared notes and moves the other notes by small steps.

<Callout title="Beginner rule of thumb" tone="harmony">
  If two chords share a note, try holding it before moving every note at once.
</Callout>
