## Syncopation accents the off-beats

In a steady 4/4 bar, beats 1 and 3 feel strong. Syncopation places accents on weak beats or between beats — the "and" of a count — which creates push and groove.

## Read a syncopated line

Count "1 and 2 and 3 and 4 and." An accent on an "and" is syncopated. Tie it across the next beat and the push gets stronger.
