## Listening practice should stay replayable

For beginners, the goal is not speed. Play the prompt, name what you notice, replay it, and then choose the label. Short loops make the memory load smaller.

<AudioExample label="Perfect fifth" notes={["C4", "G4"]} />

## Use one contrast at a time

Compare major against minor, or perfect fifth against octave. Too many choices can turn listening into guessing. The practice module keeps prompts short and visible.
