## Minor is a family, not one sound

Natural minor keeps the plain key collection. Harmonic minor raises scale degree 7. Melodic minor often raises 6 and 7 in ascending classroom examples.

<Callout title="Modes need a center" tone="melody">
  A mode is not just a set of notes. The listener needs to feel which note is acting as home.
</Callout>
