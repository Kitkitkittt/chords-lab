## Extensions stack thirds past the seventh

A triad stacks two thirds. Keep stacking and you reach the seventh, ninth, eleventh, and thirteenth. Each added tone keeps the chord's function but changes its color.

## Sus chords trade the third for a neighbor

A suspended chord replaces the third with the second (sus2) or fourth (sus4). It sounds open and unresolved until the third returns.
