## Rhythm is placement plus duration

A beat grid helps separate two questions: where does sound happen, and how long does each event last? Rests answer the first question with silence while still spending time.

<Callout title="Count the container" tone="rhythm">
  In 4/4, the measure needs four quarter-note beats of time. A rest counts toward that total.
</Callout>

## Triplets divide a space into three

A basic triplet lets three equal notes fit into a space that often holds two of the same value. The useful habit is to hear the even three-part motion before reading the symbol.
